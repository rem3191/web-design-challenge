Latitude Vs. Max Temperature

As can be expected, the weather becomes significiatly warmer as you approach the equator. Additionally the southern hemisphere will be wamrer than the northern hemisphere during this season. There is a very strong correlation between the Maximum Temperature and Latitude,in both the North and South Hemisphere. There is a steady fluctuation in the Max.
